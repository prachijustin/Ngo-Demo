We Aim to Provide Employment to All People in the state of Bihar
About GMT RealTech
GMT Realtech NGO is a company which is determined to reduce the rate of rising unemployment in the state of Bihar, India.
The NGO, at the same time, is trying to fully implement the "Nal Jal Yojana" in the state so that the people in the state of Bihar get more employed.
Also, in order to achieve maximum success in this scheme, the "Nal Jal Yojana" is working on all the divisions and levels of the state, being it a Ward level, Panchayat Level, Block Level or District Level.
To make this successful and to provide this opportunity to the people of evry corner of the state, we, at GMT Realtech NGO are providing jobs online.
Candidates, willing to do job, can filter out the jobs according to Job Position, their Qualification and Age and then apply accordingly. The applications received are then shortlisted based on the merit score of the candidate.

GMT Realtech NGO aims to provide jobs to maximum number of people and thus helping in reducing the unemployment rate of Bihar.
